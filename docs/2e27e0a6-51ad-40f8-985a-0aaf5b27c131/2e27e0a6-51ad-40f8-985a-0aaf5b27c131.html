PCI DSS Requirements

12.10.6 Develop a process to modify and evolve the incident response plan according to lessons learned and to incorporate industry developments.

Testing Procedures

12.10.6 Verify through observation, review of policies, and interviews of responsible personnel that there is a process to modify and evolve the incident response plan according to lessons learned and to incorporate industry developments.

Guidance

Incorporating “lessons learned” into the incident response plan after an incident helps keep the plan current and able to react to emerging threats and security trends.
